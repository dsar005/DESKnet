Our Story

Built for People
Who Give a Damn

DESKnet was born from a simple frustration: why did it feel impossible to find workspace accessories that were actually beautiful, well-made, and thoughtfully designed?

"Your environment shapes how you think, feel, and create. We believe everyone deserves a workspace that inspires them — not just the ones with massive budgets."
— David Chen, Founder of DESKnet
How It Started

From a Messy Desk to a Movement

In 2021, DESKnet founder David Chen was a software engineer working from a tiny Brooklyn apartment. His desk was a mess of cables, a cheap plastic laptop riser, and a cluttered surface that made deep work feel impossible.

He spent hours looking for a simple, beautiful laptop stand that wouldn't look like a plastic toy — and came up empty. Everything premium was either outrageously expensive or ugly. Everything affordable felt cheap and broke within months.

So he made his own. He spent six months prototyping an aluminum laptop stand in his spare time, shared it with 10 colleagues, and within two weeks had 50 requests from friends of friends.

DESKnet launched in January 2022 with a single product. Today, we serve 12,000+ workspace enthusiasts across 40+ countries.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Three principles guide every decision we make — from material choices to customer service.

01
Intention Over Trend

We never design by committee or chase what's trending. Every product solves a real problem our community faces. If it doesn't make your workspace meaningfully better, it doesn't get made.

02
Quality Without Compromise

We use aerospace-grade aluminum, natural cork, and aircraft-spec components. It costs more to do this — we don't care. Our customers deserve products that last years, not months.

03
Community First

Our product roadmap is built entirely on community feedback. We run monthly surveys, a Discord server with 3,000+ members, and our most loyal customers get to test every new product first.

Looking Ahead

Our Vision for the Future Workspace

The way we work has fundamentally changed. Millions of people now work from home, from coffee shops, from co-working spaces. The rigid office is gone. In its place: deeply personal, highly individual workspaces that reflect who we are.

DESKnet's vision is to become the global platform where every workspace enthusiast — from first-year students to senior engineers at top companies — can find, build, and share their perfect setup.

We're building products, content, and community to support every stage of that journey.
