Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At DESKnet, we want you to love every product you buy from us. If something isn't right, we're committed to making it right — quickly and without hassle.

1. Our 30-Day Return Guarantee

We offer a 30-day return window on all products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return it within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a return:

  • The return must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery date
  • The item must be in its original, unmodified condition
  • All original packaging, accessories, and documentation must be included
  • The item must not show signs of damage caused by misuse or accidents

Items that are not eligible for return:

  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery
  • Items damaged due to misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear
  • Items with missing original components or packaging
  • Customized or personalized items (if applicable)

3. How to Request a Return

Starting a return is simple:

  • Email hello@desknet.co with your order number and return reason (optional)
  • We'll respond within 24 hours with a prepaid return shipping label
  • Pack your item securely in its original packaging
  • Drop off the package at any UPS or USPS location
  • We'll process your refund within 1–2 business days of receiving the item

4. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 1–2 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5–10 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal: 1–3 business days
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay: 3–5 business days

You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.

5. Exchanges

Want to exchange your item for a different color, size, or product? We make exchanges easy:

  • Contact us at hello@desknet.co with your order number
  • Let us know what you'd like to exchange for
  • We'll send a prepaid return label and ship the replacement once we receive your return
  • Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly

6. Defective or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or is defective, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos showing the damage or defect
  • A brief description of the issue

We will ship a replacement immediately at no cost to you. You do not need to return the damaged item in most cases.

7. Return Shipping Costs

For standard returns, DESKnet provides a prepaid return shipping label at no cost to you. For international returns, please contact us — we handle them on a case-by-case basis and aim to make the process as smooth as possible.

8. Warranty Claims

All DESKnet products are covered by a 1-year manufacturer's warranty. If your product develops a defect within 12 months of purchase, email us with photos and your order number. We'll send a free replacement. Warranty does not cover normal wear, accidental damage, or misuse.

9. Contact Us

Questions about returns or refunds? We're here to help.

Email: hello@desknet.co
Hours: Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm EST
Response Time: Typically within a few hours during business days
