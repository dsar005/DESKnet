About the Adjustable Laptop Stand

The DESKnet Adjustable Laptop Stand is engineered for those who demand the best from their workspace. Crafted from premium aerospace-grade aluminum alloy, this stand brings your screen to eye level — eliminating neck and back strain while creating a visually clean, clutter-free desk.

Whether you're coding in a dark room, designing in a bright studio, or studying for long hours, the stand's 6 adjustable height settings (14cm – 19cm) allow you to dial in the perfect ergonomic position every time.

Key Features

  • Aerospace-grade 6061 aluminum alloy construction
  • 6 height adjustments from 14cm to 19cm
  • Foldable design — collapses to 2.4cm for portable use
  • Anti-slip silicone pads protect your desk and laptop
  • Compatible with MacBooks, Surface, Dell XPS, and all 10″–17″ laptops
  • Ventilation channels keep your laptop cool
  • Supports up to 20kg / 44lbs
  • Available in Space Gray, Silver, and Midnight Black
