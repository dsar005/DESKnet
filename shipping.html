Shipping & Returns

We ship worldwide with fast processing times. All orders come with our 30-day no-hassle return guarantee.

Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST Monday–Friday are typically shipped same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.

A tracking number will be sent to your email address once your order ships. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. Allow 24 hours for tracking information to become active after receiving your shipment confirmation.

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Window

We stand behind every product we sell. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. No questions asked.

Return Requirements

  • Item must be returned within 30 days of delivery date
  • Item must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging
  • All accessories, documentation, and components must be included
  • Items showing signs of use, damage, or modification may not be eligible

How to Initiate a Return

Email hello@desknet.co with your order number and reason for return (optional). We'll respond within 24 hours with a prepaid return shipping label and instructions. Simply pack the item securely and drop it off at any UPS or USPS location.

Refund Timeline

Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 1–2 business days. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Exchanges

Want a different color, size, or product? Contact us and we'll set up an exchange. We'll ship your replacement item as soon as we receive the original. If there's a price difference, we'll adjust accordingly.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or is defective, please email us within 7 days of delivery with photos and your order number. We'll send a replacement immediately at no cost to you.

Need Help with an Order?

Our support team is ready to help with any shipping or return questions.
